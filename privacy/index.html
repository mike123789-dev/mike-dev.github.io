Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how NoiseZen ("we", "our", or "us") handles your personal information and data. We do not collect or store any personal information.

Information We Don't Collect

NoiseZen is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your location
  • Store any user data on our servers
  • Share any information with third parties

App Functionality

NoiseZen operates entirely on your device. Any settings or preferences you configure are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Firebase, which may collect data as described in their privacy policy. We do not control the data collected by Firebase.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages as we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
